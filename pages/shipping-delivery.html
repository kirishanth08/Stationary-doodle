Shipping & Delivery

Everything you need to know about processing times, delivery windows, and tracking your Doodle Desk order.

Shipping Options

Choose the speed that fits your schedule.

Standard Shipping

3–5 business days. Free on orders over $75 within the continental United States.

Express Shipping

1–2 business days. Flat rate of $12.95, available at checkout.

International

7–14 business days to most countries. Duties and taxes calculated at checkout.

Local Pickup

Free pickup from our Springfield showroom, ready within 24 hours.

How Your Order Ships

1. Order Confirmed

You’ll receive an email confirmation the moment your order is placed.

2. Processed & Packed

Orders are picked and packed within 24 hours on business days.

3. Shipped

A tracking link is emailed as soon as your package leaves our warehouse.

4. Delivered

Track progress in real time until your order arrives at your door.

Good to Know

Address Changes

Need to update a shipping address? Contact us within 2 hours of placing your order and we’ll do our best to update it before the order ships.

Delayed or Missing Packages

Carrier delays can occasionally happen around holidays and severe weather. If your tracking hasn’t updated in 3 business days, reach out to our support team and we’ll investigate right away.

Bulk & Freight Orders

Large volume orders for schools and offices may ship via freight with a dedicated delivery window.
