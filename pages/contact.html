Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about orders and support.

Standard shipping arrives in 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping within the continental United States. You’ll receive tracking details by email as soon as your order ships. See our full Shipping & Delivery page for details.

Unopened items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in original packaging with tags attached. Contact our support team to receive a prepaid return label. Custom or personalized products are final sale unless defective. See our full Returns & Exchanges page for step-by-step instructions.

Bulk orders start at 50 units per SKU and unlock tiered discounts up to 15%. Submit a quote request on our Bulk Orders page or email hello@doodledesk.com with your product list and quantities. A dedicated account manager will respond within one business day with pricing and delivery timelines.

Reach us by phone at (555) 482-7630, email at hello@doodledesk.com, or use the contact form above. Live chat is available Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM CST. For order-specific questions, include your order number for the fastest response.
