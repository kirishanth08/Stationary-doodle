About Doodle Desk

Everything You Need to Write, Create & Learn

Our Story

Born from a passion for the written word and the creative spark, Doodle Desk began as a boutique stationery studio in 2012 — a place where premium pens met thoughtfully curated notebooks.

What started as a single storefront has grown into a trusted destination for students, artists, educators, and professionals who refuse to compromise on quality. Every product on our shelves is hand-selected, tested, and loved by our team before it reaches yours.

Today, Doodle Desk serves thousands of customers across the country — from individual creators seeking the perfect journal to schools and corporations ordering supplies in bulk. Our mission remains unchanged: elevate everyday writing and learning with tools that inspire.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted premium stationery brand — where every student, professional, and creator finds tools that elevate their work and spark their imagination.

Our Mission

To curate and deliver exceptional stationery and creative supplies with uncompromising quality, thoughtful service, and accessible pricing — empowering people to write, create, and learn without limits.

Trusted Brands
