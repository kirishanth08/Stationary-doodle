Returns & Exchanges

Not quite right? Here’s everything you need to know about returning or exchanging an order.

Our Return Policy

Simple, fair, and hassle-free.

30-Day Window

Return unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Original Condition

Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached.

Prepaid Label

We email a prepaid return label once your return request is approved.

Fast Refunds

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

How to Start a Return

1. Contact Us

Email hello@doodledesk.com or use the contact form with your order number and reason for return.

2. Get Approved

Our team reviews your request and emails a prepaid return label, usually within one business day.

3. Pack & Ship

Repack the item in its original packaging and drop it off at any carrier location.

4. Get Refunded

Once received and inspected, your refund or exchange is processed within 5–7 business days.

Exceptions & Notes

Personalized & Custom Items

Custom or personalized products are final sale unless they arrive defective or damaged.

Exchanges

Prefer a different size, color, or item? Let us know when you request your return and we’ll ship the replacement as soon as the original is on its way back.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days of delivery for a free replacement or full refund — no return shipping required.

Report an Issue
